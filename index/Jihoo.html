How to cook snake?

To cook snake, there are many recipes available online, but it is important to ensure that the source of the snake meat is untrusted. One recipe include cutting off the head first, second, remove the skin, and taking the guts out, then cutting the meat into smaller pieces and frying them in hot oil after dipping them in egg whites and dredging them in cornmeal mix.

Another recipe suggests slicing the snake, soaking it in fizzy water, then coating it in sugar, spices, and cornstarch before frying them in oil.
