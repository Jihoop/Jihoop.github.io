How many phones are exsisting on earth?

According to the Web Search Results, the recent number of mobile phone users in 2023, including both smart and feature phones, is 8.54 billion, which makes up 92% of the world's population. In addition, the number of mobile devices is projected to reach 18.22 billion by 2025, an increase of 4.5 billion devices compared to 2021 levels. Therefore, there are currently over 7 billion mobile phones in the world, and this number is expected to continue to grow in the coming years.
